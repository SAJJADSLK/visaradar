About VisaRadar24

The world's first live global visa and border intelligence platform.

Our Mission

VisaRadar24 was built to solve a problem affecting over 1.4 billion international travellers: getting accurate, real-time visa information is needlessly difficult.

Before VisaRadar24, checking visa requirements meant navigating dozens of government websites, outdated blog posts, and paid agencies — all for information that should be instantly accessible and free.

We built VisaRadar24 to be the FlightRadar24 of travel — a live, data-driven platform that makes global visa intelligence accessible to everyone, for free.

Our Data Sources

  • IATA Travel Centre — Global visa requirements database used by airlines worldwide
  • US Customs & Border Protection — Live border wait times via official API (bwt.cbp.gov)
  • UK Foreign Commonwealth Office — Travel advisories and visa updates
  • US State Department — Travel advisories and entry requirements
  • 195 Official Embassy Feeds — RSS feeds from official embassy portals
  • Henley Passport Index — Annual passport power rankings

Our Principles

✓ Real Data Only

Every data point comes from a verifiable official government source. We never invent or estimate visa requirements.

✓ Always Free

Core visa information is free to everyone. Supported by advertising, not by charging users for essential travel data.

✓ Transparent

We attribute all data sources clearly and always direct users to official sources for final verification.

✓ Privacy First

No registration required. We collect minimal data and never sell personal information to third parties.
