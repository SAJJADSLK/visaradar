Guide

Understanding Border Wait Times

Standard Lanes

For all travellers without trusted traveller programme membership. Generally longer wait times, especially during peak hours (7-9am and 4-7pm).

NEXUS/SENTRI Lanes

For pre-approved trusted travellers. Significantly shorter wait times. NEXUS for US-Canada, SENTRI for US-Mexico crossings. Membership costs $50 for 5 years.

Best Times to Cross

Early morning (6-7am) and mid-afternoon (2-3pm) on weekdays typically have the shortest wait times. Avoid Friday afternoons and Monday mornings.

Pedestrian Lanes

Walking across the border often has shorter waits than driving. Available at major crossings like San Ysidro, El Paso and Laredo.
