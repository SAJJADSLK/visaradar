Understanding Visa Types

✓ Visa Free

No visa required. You can enter the country with your passport alone. Maximum stay limits still apply — typically 30, 60, or 90 days.

✈ Visa on Arrival

You obtain your visa at the airport or border upon arrival. Usually quick — 15 to 30 minutes. A fee is typically charged in cash or card.

💻 e-Visa

Apply online before travel through the official government portal. Processing usually takes 24 hours to 5 business days. More convenient than embassy visits.

🏛 Visa Required

Must apply in advance at the embassy or consulate. Requires documentation, photos, and sometimes an interview. Allow 2–8 weeks processing time.
