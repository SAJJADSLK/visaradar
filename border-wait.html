About the data

Wait times are sourced directly from the US Customs and Border Protection (CBP) API, a public government data feed. Data represents estimated wait times for passenger vehicles at land border ports of entry. Times are refreshed approximately every 15 minutes by CBP and cached by Visa Radar for performance.

Wait time categories: Green (<30 min) · Yellow (30–60 min) · Red (>60 min).

⚠ Wait times are estimates from CBP and may not reflect real-time conditions exactly. Border wait times can change rapidly. Check bwt.cbp.gov for official CBP data.
