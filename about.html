Our mission

International travel involves navigating a complex web of visa requirements, border rules, and entry restrictions that change constantly. Most travelers rely on outdated information, word-of-mouth, or time-consuming embassy research. VisaRadar24 changes that.

We monitor official government portals, embassy RSS feeds, and public APIs from 195 countries to deliver the most current visa intelligence available anywhere — for free.

Our data sources

US CBP API

Live border wait times from US Customs and Border Protection's public data feed, updated every 15 minutes.

Embassy RSS feeds

Official RSS and Atom feeds from embassies and foreign ministries of 195 countries, monitored continuously.

Government portals

Visa rule data compiled from official government immigration portals and cross-referenced across multiple sources.

Travel advisories

Advisory data from US State Department and UK Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office.

Data accuracy & disclaimer

All visa and entry requirement data on VisaRadar24 is sourced from official public government sources and updated regularly. However, immigration rules can change rapidly, and not all changes are announced publicly in advance.

Important: VisaRadar24 provides information for general guidance purposes only. We are not a government agency, immigration law firm, or legal service. Always verify current entry requirements directly with the official embassy or consulate of your destination country before making travel arrangements.

Technology

VisaRadar24 is built as a serverless application hosted on Cloudflare's global edge network, ensuring fast load times for users worldwide. Our Cloudflare Workers infrastructure fetches and caches live data from government APIs, and our interactive map uses Leaflet.js with open map data from OpenStreetMap contributors.

All data is handled in compliance with applicable privacy laws. We do not sell user data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
