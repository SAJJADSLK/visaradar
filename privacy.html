Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective date: January 1, 2025

VisaRadar24 ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit visaradar24.com.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address — if you subscribe to policy alerts
  • Country preferences — the countries you choose to track for alerts
  • Contact form submissions — if you reach out via our contact page

Information collected automatically

  • Log data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent (collected by Cloudflare and our analytics)
  • Cookies — session cookies and consent cookies (see Cookie section below)

2. How we use your information

  • To send email alerts about visa policy changes you have opted in to receive
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our services
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the user experience
  • To respond to support requests and inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Google AdSense and advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Advertisements on this site are clearly labeled "Advertisement" and are served by Google. We do not control the content of advertisements.

4. Cookies and consent

We use the Cookiebot consent management platform (compliant with IAB TCF v2.3) to manage cookie consent. When you first visit our site, you will be presented with a consent banner. Categories of cookies used:

  • Necessary cookies — required for the site to function
  • Statistics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with the site (optional)
  • Marketing cookies — used by Google AdSense for advertising (optional, requires consent)

You may withdraw or modify your consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in our footer, or by clearing your browser cookies.

5. Third-party services

  • Cloudflare — hosting and edge network (privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacy)
  • Google AdSense — advertising (privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Cookiebot — consent management (privacy policy: cookiebot.com/privacy-policy)
  • OpenStreetMap / CartoDB — map tiles on the homepage

6. Data retention

Email addresses collected for alert subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any alert email. Log data is retained for up to 90 days.

7. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address on our contact page.

8. Children's privacy

VisaRadar24 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email and post the updated policy on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

10. Contact us

For privacy-related questions, contact us via our contact page.
